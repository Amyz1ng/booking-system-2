About Us!

What do we do?

The Pizza-berra was born just before the 2020 lockdown as a small intimate pizza restaurant for the locals of kilrush, Pizza-berra became a popular spot for town folk to come and enjoy a day out on the weekend in the countryside whilst enjoying the delicious menu, Our pizzas have been dubbed the best in the area and our ribs have rivalled some of the top rib restaurants in Co.clare. Pizza-Berra is located in the heart warming town of Kilrush, Ireland. We are a Family & Animal friendly establishment. We have a wide variety of food accross our menu arranging from starter's to desserts.Pizza is our major line at the moment and constitutes up to 80% of our total sales. Our current focus is around take-aways - easier to manage and control.

How we do it?

With our wood fired pizza oven.we offer the consumer a choice of either thick base, or traditional base (thin base) pizza. Majority of the menu is cooked in the large wood fired pizza oven and a wide variety of items for you to choose from. The menu changes seasonally and majority of the fresh is sourced locally.

Why eat with us?

Our food will cleanse anyones pallet, At Pizza-berra we strive to provide the best quality pizzas at the best value for money.Breakfasts are offered on the weekends from 8am and lunch from 12pm. If you fancy rather lounging in the gardens you can prebook one of our grazing boxes and enjoy your time in the countryside under ancient stinkwood trees or on the lush grass that surrounds the waterfall. We like to support local suppliers and have setup a small section where you can buy their produce from seasonal veg, eggs and cheese to flavoured sourdough breads. We also have a range of frozen foods for sale

interested in something special?

Our grazing boxes are freshly prepared from local and seasonal items and include a variety of the following products: Artisanal Breads, Deli Cold Cut Meats, Selection of Seasonal Fruits & Baby Veg, Locally Handcrafted Cheeses, Dips, Pestos, Pates, Preserves, Pickles, Nuts, Olives and the list goes on. Bookings are essential and must be confirmed 24 hours before arrival. You have a selection of items in your box depending on availability and what is in season. There is also an option to “bulk up” with other items such as Mini Pies, Sliders, Wraps, Pastries etc. There are a large selection of beverages available from the restaurant.
